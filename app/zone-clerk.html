⬥ Initiate Move

⬥ Prepare for Release (75%+ incoming)

Orders at 75%+ picking — prepare to receive. They will appear in Release Orders when the picker submits.

⬥ Release Orders (SuperMarket)

Pickers have submitted orders. Click Release to complete the transaction and send the order to dispatch.

⬥ Receive Inbounds

📋 Zone Receiving — two views:
• Clerk who pushed: Select your zone to see Outbounds (what you sent) and Inbounds (what's arriving to you).
• Clerk who receives: Select your zone to see Inbounds — click Receive when the pallet physically arrives.

⬥ Stocks in Zone

Live stock map for your assigned zone by SKU (pallets, units, oldest/newest pallets).

⬥ Notifications

Dedicated alert panel for your zone. Open move form directly from order-pull alerts.

⬥ What's Coming to You (Receive)

Zone clerks are pushing pallets to your zone for pickers. When they arrive, click Receive in the Zone Receiving tab.

⬥ Replenishment Warnings

Zones/SKUs at 30% stock. Acknowledge when addressed.

⬥ Orders Needing Your Zone (Push)

When another area needs stock from your zone, use the action on each line to open the move form with the right pallet and destination. Order-pull alerts (same as the bell panel) also appear at the top here.
